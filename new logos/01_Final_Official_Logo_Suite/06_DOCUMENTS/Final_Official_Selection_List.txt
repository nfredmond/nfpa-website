DESIGN DECISION SUMMARY
-----------------------
- The “N” is intentionally placed in front of the “F”.
- This was chosen because the brand reads as Nat Ford, not Ford Nat.
- The tighter overlap creates a more elegant monogram with better balance and negative space.
- The square monogram is the main icon mark.
- The circular monogram is a premium alternate badge/seal.

RECOMMENDED CORE SET
--------------------

- Primary stacked light
- Primary stacked dark
- Secondary horizontal light
- Secondary horizontal dark
- Square monogram light
- Square monogram dark
